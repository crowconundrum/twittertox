About This Project

Documenting the transformation of a platform that changed social media

Our Mission

This project aims to provide a centralized, transparent record of how Elon Musk has transformed Twitter into the platform now known as X. In addition, it provides context to help understand this transformation, the controversy surrounding it, and its real-life implications.

As one of the most significant changes to a major social media platform in recent years, the Twitter-to-X transformation deserves careful documentation and analysis that goes beyond headlines and partisan takes.

The Team

Project Origins

This project was started by a group of Class 2025 UNC Chapel Hill School of Information and Library Science students.

Our Approach

We've committed to factual accuracy, chronological clarity, and balanced presentation of this important digital transformation.

Ongoing Development

This timeline is regularly updated as new developments in the Twitter/X story unfold, ensuring a living document of this platform's evolution.

Our Methodology

We collect information from reputable news sources, official company statements, and verified social media posts to ensure accuracy. All sources are documented and available on our sources page.

Events are presented chronologically with contextual information to help understand their significance. We strive to provide a balanced view that acknowledges different perspectives on controversial developments.
