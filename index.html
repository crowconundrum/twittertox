Tracking Elon's Purchase of Twitter and its Transformation into "X"

A timeline of events, impacts, and controversies since Elon Musk's acquisition

View Timeline

Why This Matters

Elon Musk has made Twitter his own and changed the platform to suit his likes and wants. How has that affected Twitter's reputation and success? How has that affected Elon's reputation and his other companies?

How has the market valuation and usage of the platform been affected? And how has Elon's friendship with Donald Trump progressed since buying the platform? With this timeline, you can track this and much more.

Timeline of Events

April 2022

April 2022: Twitter Acquisition Announcement

Elon Musk's $44 billion offer to buy Twitter marks one of the largest tech acquisitions in history.

October 2022

October 2022: Deal Completion

After months of legal battles, the acquisition was finally completed on October 27, 2022.

November 2022

November 2022: Verification System Changes & Mass Layoffs

Twitter Blue's $8 verification system launch leads to widespread impersonation issues and temporary suspension. Mass layoffs ensue.

July 2023

July 2023: Rebranding to X

Twitter is rebranded as "X", marking the end of the iconic blue bird logo and 17-year-old brand name.

August 2023

August 2023: CCDH Lawsuit & Block Feature Changed

X files a lawsuit against the Center for Countering Digital Hate. The block feature is reworked.

Early 2024

Early 2024: Valuation and User Changes

X's valuation drops to $15 billion, showing significant decline in users and ad revenue.

March 2024

March 2024: X Loses Lawsuit

A federal court rules against X in its case against the Center for Countering Digital Hate.

Impact of the Transformation

User Experience

Changes to algorithms and content moderation have led to significant differences in user experience.

Business Model

Advertiser exodus and new subscription models have impacted platform revenue and valuation.

Free Speech

Musk's "absolute free speech" stance has sparked debates about platform responsibility and moderation.

Musk's Other Businesses

Time and attention spent managing X has affected performance and market perception of his other companies.
